1. ～ないでください (〜naide kudasai) - Formal way to request someone not to do something.
2. ～なければ なりません (〜nakereba narimasen) - "Must do" or "have to do".
3. ～てしまう (〜te shimau) - Used to express regret or completion of an action.
4. ～ては いけません (〜tewa ikemasen) - "Must not do" instructions.
5. ～なくてもいい (～nakute mo ii) - Expressing permission not to do something.
6. ～てみる (〜temiru) - Attempting or trying to do something.
7. し (shi) - Used to list reasons or factors.
8. ～てもいい (〜temo ii) - Giving permission to do something. 
9. ～ば (〜ba) - If or when in English.
10. ～たら (〜tara) - Also used as if or when, more like an "after" in English. 
11. ～たり (〜tari) - To do things like.
12. ～ほど (〜hodo) - To express the extent of something.
13. ～そう (〜sou) - Heard that, Appear, Seem.
14. ～てから (〜te kara) - After doing.
15. ～すぎる (－sugiru) - To express an action/state being done excessively.
16. ～前に (〜mae ni) - Before doing.
17. ～とき (〜toki) - When.
18. ～ながら (〜nagara) - While, during.
19. 〜くない(〜kunai) - Negative adjective form.
20. 〜すぎる (‐sugiru) - Expressing excessive action or state.
21. 〜ように/ような (〜you ni / you na) - Used to indicate similarity, manner, purpose.
22. 〜たい (—tai) - Expressing desire to do something.
23. 〜はず (〜hazu) - Expected to, supposed to.
24. ～まで (～made) - Until/to the extent of.
25. 〜てあげる (〜te ageru)/ 〜てくれる (〜te kureru)/ 〜てもらう (〜te morau) - Giving and receiving actions.
26. 〜方 (〜hou or 〜kata) - Way of doing something.
27. 〜ぬ (〜nu) - Expressing negativity, old-fashioned way.
28. 〜なら (〜nara) - If, in case of.
29. 〜もの/こと (〜mono/koto) - Nominalizers.
30. 〜られる (〜rareru) - Potential form.
31. 〜させる (〜saseru) - Causative form.
32. 〜させられる (〜saserareru) - Causative passive form. 
33. 〜と (〜to) - Quotation.
34. 〜など (〜nado) - Such as.
35. 〜のなら (〜nonara) - If it is the case.
36. 意味 (imi) - Meaning.
37. 可能形 (kanoukei) - Potential form.
38. 比較 (hikaku) - Comparison.
39. 直説法 (chokusetsuho) - Direct method.
40. 過去形 (kakokei) - Past tense.
41. 過去完了形 (kakokanryo-kei) - Past perfect tense.
42. 未来形 (miraikei) - Future tense.
43. 現在形 (genzaikei) - Present tense.
44. 現在進行形 (genzai shinkokei) - Present progressive tense.

These are some of the grammar points necessary for JLPT N4 level Japanese. This may not be the full list but covers most points for the standard study guide.
